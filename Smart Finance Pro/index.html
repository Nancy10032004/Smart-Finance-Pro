Financial Health Calculation

The **Financial Health Score** is computed dynamically out of 100 points based on the following three financial metrics:

1. Monthly Savings Rate (40 Points Max)

The percentage of income saved this month: `((Income - Expense) / Income) * 100`.

  • **40 points** for a savings rate >= 30%
  • **30 points** for 20% to 30%
  • **20 points** for 10% to 20%
  • **5 to 10 points** for < 10%

2. Budget Compliance (30 Points Max)

Discipline in staying within your configured category budgets.

  • **30 points** if no category budgets are breached.
  • **15 points** if 1 category budget is breached.
  • **0 points** if multiple category budgets are breached.

3. Income-to-Expense Compliance (30 Points Max)

Ensures current monthly income fully covers expenditure.

  • **30 points** if net balance is positive (Income > Expenses).
  • **0 points** if you spent more than you earned (deficit).

Keeping a score above **85** keeps you in the **Excellent** tier, helping you reach your long-term wealth goals faster!
